Each node represents a specific genetic variant, and the node size corresponds to the number of mutations within that variant. The connections between nodes (edges) represent shared mutations between variants. The thickness of each edge is determined by the degree of shared mutations, providing a continuous mapping of genetic similarity. This network offers a clear and intuitive way to explore relationships and commonalities among variants based on their mutation profiles.
